Override authority rules
Who can override
Governance committee only — your dispute triggers a formal committee review. You cannot grant the override yourself.
What's required
Your written rationale. Committee reviews both positions. Written decision with dissenting notes preserved.
What cannot be overridden
The audit trail itself. Hard blocks rooted in a direct legal prohibition. The visibility of the open block to all role holders.
